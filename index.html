Our Services

1. Groundwater Hydro-Geological Survey

Before borehole drilling, it's very important to conduct a Hydro-Geological survey in your proposed site to ascertain suitable sites for exploration of significant quantities of groundwater. Common methods used include Vertical Electrical Sounding (V.E.S) and Horizontal Electrical Profiling (H.E.P).

2. Borehole Drilling

Utilizing the latest technology and machinery, we provide efficient and precise borehole drilling services for industrial, commercial, and domestic needs, ensuring optimal water access.

3. Borehole Maintenance

Regular maintenance is key to the longevity and efficiency of your borehole. We offer comprehensive servicing, repairs, and preventative measures to keep your water supply flowing reliably.

4. Borehole Equipping

Borehole equipping consists of pump installation with its accessories such as control panel, submersible and underground cables, galvanized pipes, and main connection to the tank. We ensure a complete and functional setup.

5. Tank Installation

Our tank installations consist of welded steel containers supported by multiple welded steel legs, perimeter columns, and a central welded steel rise. These are reinforced with horizontal struts and diagonal bracing rods for maximum stability.
